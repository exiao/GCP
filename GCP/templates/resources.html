Green Checklist
  • The Green Checklist is the basis of ASUC GCP. This document outlines the structure of the program and organizes categories in which student groups are able to reduce their waste. The Checklist also offers the ability for groups to create new ways in which they can become more sustainable via the Miscellaneous portion of the guide.
